My name is Joe and I am a sticky element

This is the story of me and how I got sticky!

What happened to me.

That fateful day!

As I said, my name is Joe and I am one of the elements. You know, earth, wind, fire, water, and me Joe. I am not sure what kind of element I am, I'm not hot or cold, windy, or rainy. I guess I'm Joey. Anyway, there had been a rumor going around that the elements, fire and water were dating in secret. I guess it's true because a few weeks ago they announced they were getting married! Fire had chosen me to be best man and therefore, I was in charge of the bachelor party. After some planning, I finally found a place to hold it.It was a gentleelements club so you can imagine what kind of natural disaster might happen there. There were two exotic animal dancers named Sandy and Stormy and they promised a good time. It started out tame enough, but eventually things got a little wild. Honestly, the last thing I remember was doing ten counts on a keg of firewater.I must have passed out and somehow we must have made a roadtrip to disney world because when I woke up my face was in a giant jar of honey and Winnie the Pooh was asleep on the floor. Needless to say, I have been a sticky element ever since.Thank you for reading my story and if you would like to help me get unsticky, please scroll up to the top, it should help!
